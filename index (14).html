Colonel Alex Bruton · U.S. Army

Colonel Alex Bruton is a decorated United States Army officer and qualified Computer Engineer with over 27 years of distinguished service. Born in 1974, he has dedicated the better part of his life to the defense of his nation — rising through the ranks through grit, technical mastery, and an unrelenting commitment to those who serve alongside him.

A Ranger-qualified officer with deep expertise in special operations, military technology systems, and strategic command, Colonel Bruton has led soldiers through some of the most demanding combat environments on earth. His deployments to Iran, Afghanistan, and Oman gave him firsthand experience in diverse and complex operational theaters, where his engineering background proved as vital as his combat leadership. He currently serves on an active defense contract, continuing to protect and serve his nation in a senior advisory capacity.

In his personal life, he has faced battles just as demanding as any on the battlefield. A widower, he lost his beloved wife to cancer several years ago — a loss that deepened his resolve and his compassion. He is the proud father of a 17-year-old daughter, whom he raises with the same dedication and love he brings to everything he does. Off duty, he is known as a mentor of character, a tireless advocate for veterans and their families, and a living example of the values he has spent a lifetime defending.
